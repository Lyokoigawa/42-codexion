mandatory arguments:

number_of_coders - number of coders + dongles

time_to_burnout (ms) - time until a coder goes into burnout.
time is reset per compile

time_to_compile - time it takes to compile. a coder must hold 2 dongles

time_to_debug - time spent debugging

time_to_refactor - time it takes to refactor. once finished,
a coder will begin searching for dongles to start compiling

number_of_compiles_required - simulation stops once this number is reached,
or a coder burns out

dongle_cooldown - after being released a dongle is
unavailable after it's been used

scheduler - the arbitation policy used by the dongles to decide who gets them.
either FIFO or EDF.
FIFO - first in, first out: dongle is granted to the coder who arrived first in the queue
EDF - earliest deadline first: deadline = last_compile_start + time_to_burnout

coder number 1 sits next to coder number_of_coders. any others sit next to N + 1 and N - 1

phases: compile -> debug -> refactor -> compile




if there's only 1 coder, only 1 dongle will be needed to compile

coders use dongles on their left and right sides, not "any" dongles like in a pile

coders will ONLY grab 2 dongles at a time
